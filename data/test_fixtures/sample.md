# Introduction to Biology

This is an introductory section about biology.

## Cell Structure

Cells are the basic unit of life. They contain:
- Nucleus
- Mitochondria
- Cell membrane

### Prokaryotic Cells

Prokaryotic cells lack a nucleus.

### Eukaryotic Cells

Eukaryotic cells have a membrane-bound nucleus.

## Genetics

Genetics is the study of heredity and variation.
